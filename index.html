About us

We are your unofficial Helping Hand

Have a Business idea?

Got a business idea you're itching to explore? Look no further! SDEC offers assistance and resources for aspiring student entrepreneurs. From business planning to networking opportunities, we're here to help you turn your entrepreneurial dreams into reality.

Have a dream project?

Have a passion project or a big idea you've been dreaming about? SDEC is here to help you bring your dreams to life. Whether it's a creative endeavor, a social cause, or a personal goal, we provide the resources, guidance, and support to help you succeed.

Want to learn while working?

Gain hands-on experience and practical skills by actively participating in SDEC activities. Whether you're working on a project, collaborating with a team, or organizing an event, you'll learn valuable lessons that will benefit you in your academic and professional journey.

Want to Organise Event or Take any initiative?

Take the lead in organizing events and initiatives that matter to you and your peers. Whether it's a workshop, seminar, or community project, SDEC provides the platform and support to turn your ideas into reality.

Want to grow and help others?

Join us at SDEC to grow personally and professionally while making a positive impact on others. We offer opportunities for personal development, mentorship, and community involvement.

Have Questions?

Frequently asked questions

What is SDEC?
SDEC stands for Student Development & Empowerment Community. It is an independent student body dedicated to supporting students in various aspects of their personal and professional growth.
What does SDEC offer?
SDEC offers assistance with business ideas, dream projects, event organization, and initiatives. We provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for students to develop their skills and make a positive impact.
Who can join SDEC?
SDEC is open to all students who are passionate about personal development, entrepreneurship, community engagement, and making a difference. Whether you're an undergraduate or graduate student, everyone is welcome to join our community.
How can I get involved with SDEC?
Getting involved with SDEC is easy! You can join as a member and participate in our events, workshops, and projects. Additionally, you can take on leadership roles, contribute ideas, or volunteer your time to help shape the direction of our community.
How can SDEC benefit me?
SDEC offers numerous benefits, including opportunities to network, develop new skills, gain practical experience, and receive support and guidance from fellow students and mentors. By being part of SDEC, you can enhance your personal and professional growth while making meaningful connections within the community.
Is SDEC associated with KIIT?
No, SDEC is started by students of KIIT but it is not associate or affiliated with KIIT in any manner. It's a completely independent student body.
