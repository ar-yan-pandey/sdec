Growth & Development

SDEC Project Forge offers live classes across various fields such as tech, marketing, finance, corporate culture, and personality development. Participants will engage in sessions with industry experts through interactive talks and speaker sessions, gaining a diverse skill set to tackle academic and professional challenges.

Networking

Project Forge prioritizes networking by connecting participants with peers and industry experts through sessions and shows. This direct interaction with real businesses and startups helps expand professional networks, offering valuable insights and potential collaborations for future growth.

Real Life Experience

Exceptional participants in the Project Forge community will be given opportunities to work on real-life projects, businesses, and startups. These hands-on roles, curated by SDEC’s Hyperlink Team, provide practical experience in both non-paid and paid positions, enhancing professional development and career readiness.

What is Project Forge?

The Real One stop Solution

Project Forge is the latest initiative by SDEC, designed as an open community for all students—whether in college, school, or graduates. This program is intended to be a comprehensive solution for skill and personality development. Through live classes, talks, and speaker sessions, Project Forge aims to provide real-world working experience in actual projects, startups, and businesses. It also facilitates networking and growth opportunities.

Have Questions?

Frequently asked questions

When does Project Forge start?
Project Forge begins on 1st August 2024.
Is there any cost to join Project Forge?
No, Project Forge is completely free for all members and will remain free forever.
What kind of skills can I learn in Project Forge?
You can learn a variety of skills including tech, marketing, finance, corporate culture, communication, time management, and other personality development skills.
Can members request specific topics to learn?
Yes, members can request topics they want to learn, and Project Forge will strive to fulfill these requests.
Will Project Forge only focus on technical skills?
No, Project Forge will cover both technical skills and personality skills, such as communication and time management, to ensure holistic development.
Who can join Project Forge?
Project Forge is open to all students, whether they are in college, school, or even graduates. It is an open community designed for comprehensive skill development and growth.
